Our Standard

Responsible Care, Clean Facilities, and True Love for Dogs

At Puppies in the Village, we believe that every dog deserves the same care you would want for a family member. Our standards aren't just slogans — they are part of the daily routine in our dog family. 
Our breeding home is family-oriented in nature, the dogs grow up in a unique rural atmosphere with a huge yard for walks and energy release. The proper exposure and natural environment contribute to the physical and mental health of the puppies

  • Daily Attention

    Every dog in our boarding facility receives personal attention throughout the day with walks, feeding at regular hours, and warm human contact that reduces separation anxiety, while maintaining their overall health. We ensure healthy and quality nutrition, routine veterinary care such as vaccinations and deworming, and of course availability for emergency treatments.

  • Safe and Clean Facilities

    Our facility is maintained according to strict cleaning protocols. Dogs stay in spacious, ventilated and sanitized spaces, because your dog's health is our top priority.

  • Experience with Dogs at All Life Stages

    From newborn puppies to senior dogs - our team is skilled in caring for varying needs, including dogs with special sensitivities, different breeds, and diverse temperaments.

  • Reliability and Personalized Service

    We work with small and large breeds and offer reliable and professional service. Additionally, we also offer home delivery option for your convenience, as part of our service tailored to your needs.
